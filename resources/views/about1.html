About Us

We are a group of educational enthusiasts who are passionate about making the best quality knowledge across different fields available to students all over Africa. Aeolus Mini classroom is one of Nigeria’s fastest growing online educational platforms serving a multitude of needs across Africa of both tutors and students. It’s about creating a central place for student and teachers. Bringing learning remotely to enhance the educational sector, this way there would be no restrictions of distance to learning. Irrespective of where the students might be, they would always have access to the best education.

our mission

Leveraging technology to create solutions that help the educational sector thrive. We plan to revolutionize the educational sector, providing the best quality of education irrespective of the barrier of distance, that will give both the tutor and student a platform to be expressive and learn wide with no restrictions and set a high standard across different countries. This would also help the teachers make an impact while earning a living.

WHY MINI CLASSROOM

Mini Classroom makes learning easier and accessible, it removes the limitation of distance in having good education. Students are encouraged to take this opportunity in vast learning and enhancing their skills.

Aeolus Mini Classroom the home for hungry minds and vast learners.
